Module 1

Movement

Training that fits your life. You don't need to be in the gym 5 days a week to see results.

The Approach

This isn't about grinding yourself into the ground. It's about building a consistent movement habit that raises your baseline and keeps you performing at work and at home. Three quality sessions a week beats six half-arsed ones every time.

Module 2

Nutrition

Eat well without it consuming your life. Simple principles, flexible execution.

The Approach

You don't need a complicated diet. You need enough protein, the right calories for your goal, and a way of eating you can actually stick to. The tools below help you figure out exactly what that looks like for you.

Module 3

Recovery

The module most people skip. It's also the one that unlocks everything else.

The Approach

Sleep, stress management, and breathwork aren't soft skills — they're performance tools. If you're running on 5 hours and three coffees, no amount of training or nutrition will fix that. Start here.

Use this daily. 4 counts in, 4 hold, 4 out, 4 hold. Do it before a big meeting, after a hard workout, or before bed. Two minutes changes your state.
